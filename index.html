The Story

A tale of friendship, courage, and determination to save Central Park!

Brave Birds vs. Evil Cats

Evil cat Capones and his gang are planning to destroy New York's Central Park to build a factory. Our brave bird friends Kevin, Rosie, and Bob must protect the park and its landmarks!

The cunning Capones will stop at nothing to achieve their goals, but our heroes have something more powerful than money and influence – friendship and brotherhood!

Our best friends need all their ingenuity to play 3D matches, solve quests, and uncover the games of the insidious cats. Your decisions can save our best friends from the dangerous cat and his angry servants!

Join us in this epic battle!

Meet the Characters

Get to know the brave birds who are fighting to save Central Park!

Kevin

The Leader

Kevin is brave, strategic, and always has a plan. As the leader of the bird trio, he coordinates their efforts against the evil cats.

Rosie

The Genius

Rosie is the brains of the operation. Her quick thinking and problem-solving skills help the birds overcome the cats' traps.

Bob

The Strongman

Bob may not be the sharpest beak in the nest, but his strength and unwavering loyalty make him an essential part of the team.

The Villain

Capones the Cat

The Evil Mastermind

Capones is a wealthy and ruthless business cat who cares only about profit. He plans to destroy Central Park to build his factory, regardless of the consequences for the environment and the park's inhabitants.

With his gang of feline henchmen, Capones will stop at nothing to achieve his goals. But he didn't count on the determination of three brave birds who are ready to defend their home!
